Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 March 2026

StockUp is operated by SpendWise Apps ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what information we collect when you use the StockUp app ("the App") and how we use it.

Information We Collect

StockUp does not require you to sign up with an email, name, or any personal information. We collect the following:

  • Anonymous identifier — a randomly generated ID created on your device, used to sync your data across sessions. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Watchlist and shopping list — the products you choose to save, synced to our servers so they persist across sessions.
  • Push notification token — if you opt in to price alerts, we store a device token to deliver notifications.
  • Feedback and product reports — if you choose to submit feedback or report incorrect data, we store your message along with basic device information (device model, OS version, app version) to help us investigate issues.
  • Crash and error data — if the App crashes, diagnostic information is sent to our error monitoring service to help us fix bugs.
  • Technical data — when the App communicates with our servers, your IP address and standard request headers may be automatically logged by our infrastructure providers as part of normal server operations.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • Sync your watchlist and shopping list
  • Send price drop notifications for watchlisted products (opt-in only)
  • Respond to feedback and fix reported product data issues
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and bugs

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Sentry — crash and error monitoring
  • Expo — push notification delivery
  • Apple Push Notification Service / Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notification delivery on iOS and Android respectively

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share anonymised, aggregated data (such as overall product popularity trends) with third parties, but this data cannot be used to identify individual users.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements from third-party ad networks. These providers may independently collect device advertising identifiers and interaction data through their own SDKs, in accordance with their own privacy policies. We may use anonymised, aggregated usage data (such as product category interests) to help serve more relevant advertisements, but we will never share information that can identify you personally. You can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as you use the App. You can delete individual items from your watchlist and shopping list at any time. To request deletion of all data associated with your account, contact us at spendwiseapps@gmail.com and we will process your request within 30 days.

Please note that because accounts are anonymous, uninstalling the App or clearing its data will permanently disconnect you from your existing data, and we will have no way to link it back to you.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. No identifying information is required to use the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
